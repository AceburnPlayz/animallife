Birds

Birds are feathered, warm-blooded vertebrates known for their ability to fly. They are found in diverse habitats, including forests, deserts, and oceans. Birds exhibit a wide range of sizes, shapes, and colors, and they have adaptations such as beaks and wings that enable them to fulfill various ecological roles.
